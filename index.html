About The Oasis

This newly remodeled, 3 bedroom, 3 bathroom house, has an in-ground heated saltwater pool and is located mid-island in a quiet residential area with the beautiful Gulf right across the street. It can accommodate 6 people with plenty of living space to relax and unwind. Best of all, this home has 13' sliding doors that open to the lanai where you can dine or just take in the view of this gorgeous tropical setting!

The location is a 2 minute walk to Ft. Myers Beach and the beautiful Gulf. Your tropical paradise maintains its small island charm away from the hustle and bustle of Times Square, thus affording quiet relaxation for that desired relaxing getaway. It is equipped with all the amenities you are accustomed to at home, PLUS the tropical setting!
