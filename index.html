About The Oasis

Welcome to "The Oasis" at 5612 Easy Street. Walking into this stunning 5 bedrooms, 4 bathroom home you will notice the attention to detail that the owners have taken to make this an extraordinary vacation rental. The house is just the beginning though. It is literally an oasis, and if so desired, you wouldn't even have to leave it due to all the amenities available, including and in-ground heated saltwater pool. The home basically has two completely separate living accommodations, perfect for large or extended families who want to be together yet have privacy when desired. Sleeping a maximum of 12 guests total between the upper and lower units, it provides two complete kitchens, two complete in-home laundry facilities, and two separate climate control units. The mid-island location is perfectly nestled in a quiet residential area with a 2 minute walk to Ft. Myers Beach and the beautiful Gulf of Mexico. Your tropical paradise maintains its small island charm away from the hustle and bustle of Times Square, thus affording quiet relaxation for that desired relaxing getaway. It is equipped with all the amenities you are accustomed to at home, PLUS the tropical setting!
